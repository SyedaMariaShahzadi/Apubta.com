APUBTA History

 

The BPS faculty in all universities of Pakistan, which constitue more than 88% of the total faculty, is facing various issues of promotion and job structure. To address the issues of all BPS faculty, an idea of All Pakistan Universities BPS Teachers Association (APUBTA) was coined by Dr. Hazir Rahman, Associate Professor, Department of Microbiology, Abdul Wali Khan University Mardan and Mr. Muddasar Abass, Lectutrer from Kohat University of Science and Technology.   Dr. Hazir took the initiative to lead on social and print media. A first formal online meeting was held on June 8, 2020 in which Dr. Hazir Rahman , Dr. Imtiaz Ahmad, Dr. Hidayat Ullah Khan, Dr. Muhammad Asif, Dr. Sami ur Rahman, Dr. Ijaz Ahmad, Dr. Musaadiq Ibrahim, Dr. Noor Jehan, Dr. Zia ul Islam, Dr. Sheraz M. Khan , Dr. Muhammad, Dr. Ubaid and some other colleagues participated.
After thoroughly exploring the different options it was concluded that for the interim cabinet Dr. Sami ur Rahman from University of Malakand was elected as President APUBTA and Dr. Hidayat from Abbotabad University of Science & Technology as General Secretary APUBTA. After the first meeting both the Honorable President and GS took the lead and with the help of core committee especially Dr. Imtiaz Ahmad from University of Malakand and Dr. Zia ul Haq from University of the Punjab, who selflessly contribute for the cause of APUBTA on each and every forum.
APUBTA is now a growing tree which is recognized and acceptable in concerned quarters. Soon inshallah APUBTA will deliver more on ground for the dignity of BPS faculty….
