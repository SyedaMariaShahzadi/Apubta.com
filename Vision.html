Vision
To provide medium of cooperation and facilitation to all stakeholders for the betterment of BPS faculty.
Mission

To safeguard and promote the interests of the university BPS teachers and to build conducive academic environment in universities
Objectives

 

• To promote knowledge so as to form a “Commonwealth of knowledge” for BPS teachers and, for this purpose to arrange talks,     lectures seminars, symposia, conferences and conventions.

• To promote research and to strive for raising the standard of teaching and for reorganizing it on sound lines to meet the     contemporary challenges.


• To secure representation for the members in the various governing bodies of the Universities and in other bodies concerning the     affairs of the universities generally.


• To cooperate with and render all possible legal help to the BPS teachers of all Pakistani Universities to ensure its smooth and     efficient administration for enhancing its prestige and the honor and integrity of the members and for the welfare of the staff and     students.


• To promote unity, brotherly relations, mutual understanding, tolerance and national outlook among the members, and for this     purpose to organize social and cultural activities with relevant forums and bodies.


•  To secure and to protect and safeguard the genuine rights of the members generally and to defend any action for that purpose        at the administrative level or in the course of law, and to meet the expenses involved and to compensate any loss that any     member/office-bearer or representative may suffer in discharging his duties as such to the extent that may be possible.


• To consider matters concerning the mode of appointment and the terms and conditions of service of the members and the   problems relating to their appointmentâ€™s confirmations, annual increment, promotions, transfers, study leave, scholarships,    residential accommodation and financial matters and to take necessary/codal measures against any discriminatory or illegal      action(s).


•  To cooperate with the national and international organizations of teachers and with other organizations in promoting the cause of     education.


•  To develop contacts with the students with a view to advise and guide them in their curricular and co-curricular activities.


•  To consider generally all matters concerning the welfare of the members and of their dependents and if required, taking necessary     measures as aforementioned in any such matter.


•  To undertake such other activities as the Association may decide to achieve its objectives.


•  To strive for the due and legitimate rights of uniform time scale promotion and salaries at par with other teachers and employees     working in the same institution with the same qualification and workload.
